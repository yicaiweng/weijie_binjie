About

Once upon a time.. there was a young faire lady Binjie and well-behaved gentleman Weijie who met via a mutual friend. They went to Ramin Karmloo's concert, talked hours on the phones, messaged each other on daily basis. And it was the badminton racket for Binjie's birthday and the Broadway show 'Les Miserables' made them fall in love.. Today, I stand by their side, and withness their love story continues... and live their happily life.. ----- Yicai Weng 02/25/17
